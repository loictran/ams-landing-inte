Frequently asked questions

Who can join the AMS platform?

The AMS is open to all Aesthetic and Anti-Aging Medicine professionals: physicians and non-physicians alike. Our members include dermatologists, plastic surgeons, aesthetic practitioners, students and residents, industry representatives, clinical staff, and more.

What are the membership types?

You can join the AMS as a free or premium member, based on your needs.
Both membership types give you access to high-quality educational content and networking opportunities with aesthetic professionals around the world.
Additionally, AMS Premium unlocks CPD/CME accredited content, exclusive member discounts, and a library of videos on demand (VOD) from the entire IM-Aesthetics series of events - highly-reputed international conferences with top-notch scientific programs and the world's best speakers.

What is included with free membership?

When you register for free, you will join the online AMS community, where you can ask questions, discuss experiences, and interact with your peers around the world.
You will also gain access to a selection of free resources, including scientific webinars, AMS Original content, demonstration videos, and AMS TV (social media talks, interviews), as well as AMS partner publications (PRIME Journal and The Aesthetic Guide)..

What are the benifits of being a premium member?

With AMS Premium, you will benefit from all the features of free membership, plus additional benefits and academic content designed to support your professional development. This includes:

CONFERENCE VIDEO ON-DEMAND
Watch videos on demand (VOD) from the entire IM-Aesthetics series of global events, including the AMWC 2023 conference at your own pace on. The live-streamed scientific sessions and a selection of sponsored workshops will be posted on the AMS platform a few days after the event. This top-tier scientific video content is available exclusively to AMS Premium Members.

PREMIUM EDUCATION, 365 DAYS A YEAR
• Get full access to the AMS library with 100s of premium academic videos: presentations from global conferences, scientific webinars, live demos, and interviews with key opinion leaders. The library is continually updated with new content, hand-picked by our dedicated team and available 24/7.
• Claim your CME points and CPD certificate on selected content, all year long.

EXCLUSIVE DISCOUNTS
Premium members enjoy preferential rates for the IM-Aesthetics series of events worldwide, including AMWC Monaco. You will also benefit from a 20% discount on best-selling books from the AMS store.

CONFERENCE VIDEO ON-DEMAND
Be recognized as a premium member of the fastest growing society in Aesthetic and Anti-Aging Medicine: receive a personalized certificate of premium membership and an AMS stamp to display on your website.

What is included with free membership?

AMS Premium Membership can be purchased as a one-time payment of 290 Euros/350 USD for 12 months. The membership renews automatically each year. You can cancel it by sending a request at least two months prior to the end of your first year commitment. For more information, consult our Membership Terms & Conditions.
